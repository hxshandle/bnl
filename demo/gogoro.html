The Aeroframe ™ Revolution

Ultra lightweight. Incredibly Strong. More Agile. When no existing frame would do, we looked to the space age aluminum chassis designs revolutionizing racetracks and supercars the world-over. Then we built our own that’s 30% lighter and 2X stronger than any scooter before.

100% Aluminum

Every gram saved equals more power to the road. That added performance starts with a single stamped sheet of racing-grade aluminum alloy. We’re not only far lighter than traditional frame scooters; we’re recyclable, so our footprint is lighter too.

Stiffer

Our uncommonly agile ride owes its responsiveness to a remarkable Aeroframe™ chassis. Each component is precision bonded and heat tempered to deliver a seamless core that distributes force evenly across the frame, then right back onto the road where it belongs.

Stronger

City roads can be punishing so we built our chassis to take whatever comes. Engineered for incredible durability, we use fewer parts, each locking tightly into a unified whole. The result is our Aeroframe™ Aluminum Monocoque Chassis built with the same standards as the world’s most famous supercars.

Better

Every detail of our revolutionary Aeroframe™ chassis makes riding more exciting. We’ve streamlined, refined and reimagined every component to deliver what no other scooter can. You get more power, more control and a ride that’s simply more fun.

Part Jet. Part Racecar. Altogether New.

Inspired by the fastest vehicles on earth. Our high-performance suspension combines the best from jet landing gear with Formula 1 racing designs. The result is an unbelievably smooth, responsive ride that feels closer to flying than riding.

Rear Suspension

Incredible control relies on constant contact with the road. Our four point race car inspired multi link suspension design maintains superior tire/road cohesion, delivering new levels of stability and control. All four points pivot in unison as you carve through traffic – never skipping a beat.

Front Suspension

Inspired by aircraft landing gear, our advanced design is the innovative inverted single piston, single arm suspension in the scooter industry. You’ll feel new levels of response and control as you take on winding roads and fly through even the toughest rush hour.
